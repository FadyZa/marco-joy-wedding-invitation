OUR LOVE STORY

How It All Began

Joy:

Our love story began like a beautiful fairytale when Marko sent me a friend request on Facebook. What started as a simple, innocent chat soon blossomed into something magical — a deep, soul-stirring connection that made us inseparable. We quickly became the most important parts of each other's worlds, two hearts beating in perfect harmony.

On January 3rd, 2022, we officially began our journey as a couple, and from that day forward, we chose each other every single morning. Despite the doubts and whispers from others, our love only grew stronger, deeper, and more passionate with time. After three extraordinary years filled with tender support, endless laughter, and unwavering devotion, he made my dreams come true on January 7th, 2025, when he got down on one knee and proposed.

That moment — when he looked into my eyes and asked me to be his forever — remains the most breathtaking, heart-melting experience of my life. It was pure magic, overflowing with joy, gratitude, and a love so profound it brought tears to my eyes. I will cherish that beautiful memory in my heart for the rest of eternity. ❤️

What's your most memorable date?

Marco: One of the most deeply romantic and touching dates we shared was the day we went out together right after my exams. I was completely exhausted and stressed, carrying a heavy secret in my heart — something I believed could have ended our relationship long before it truly began.

Yet when I opened up and told Joy everything, she didn't pull away. Instead, she received my words with such gentle calm, pure grace, and unconditional love that my fears melted away. There was no fight, no drama — only understanding and warmth that wrapped around my soul like a soft embrace.

We wandered, ate delicious food, shared sweet drinks, and laughed until our hearts felt light again. In that moment, I realized how beautifully strong our love is — how it can turn even the hardest days into something magical and unforgettable.

It remains one of the most precious and romantic times I've ever spent with Joy. ❤️
